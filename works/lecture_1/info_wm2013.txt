1st round data of 2013 Wimbledon men's singles

Original: Wimbledon-men-2013.csv
https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Tennis+Major+Tournament+Match+Statistics

Variables contained:
Result - Result of the match (0/1) 1:winner, 0:loser
FNG - Final Number of Games
FSP - First Serve Percentage
FSW - First Serve Won by player
ACE - Aces won by player 
WNR - Winners earned by player
UFE - Unforced Errors committed by player
NPW - Net Points Won by player
